About OrbitLinked

Building Ghana's
Connectivity Backbone

OrbitLinked Network Ltd is a Ghana-based satellite connectivity infrastructure company on a mission to eliminate broadband inequality across West Africa. We deploy enterprise-grade satellite broadband to the institutions and communities that need it most — where fibre hasn't reached and mobile networks fall short.

We operate a partnership-led model, working alongside satellite operators, ISPs, government bodies, and NGOs to deploy affordable, managed broadband at scale. Our infrastructure-first approach means we're building something lasting.
